Get Involved in FDC3

The FDC3 standards are the result of the work of a dedicated group of contributors from both FINOS members and non-member contributing organizations alike. Ready to get involved in our community? Here are some ways to get started:

Collaborate with the Community

Most FDC3 collaboration takes place in the FDC3 GitHub repository, where you can raise issues, submit pull requests and view meeting minutes.

Learn more about the standard

Join the FDC3 list and check out the archives to stay up to date with project. You can also join the quarterly FDC3 general meeting: you can find meeting details in the FINOS Project calendar

Participate in the standard ideation

The Standards working group meets once a month to shape the next version of the FDC3 standard: you can find meeting details in the FINOS Project calendar. If you'd like to formally enroll as a voting Standard participant, see please follow these instructions.

Are you using FDC3?

If you an existing individual or corporate user of the FDC3 standard, we would love to hear from you: just email the FDC3 General List with details about how you are using the standard. If you'd like to be listed as a standard user in our homepage, you can directly send a pull request to update our website or, if listing your logo publicly requires legal evaluation, you can reach out privately to the FDC3 Product Management Committee.
